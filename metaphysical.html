Metaphysical

the science of what is beyond the physical

  • Meta
    1. Greek: among, with, after, more comprehensive, transcending
    2. Contemporary: an x about x

what

Meta began with the Greeks and was utilized as a means to describe something that comes after. A claim that Aristotle's Metaphysics was named thus because it was the book which he wrote following Physics.
Since then, metaphysics has been understood as the study of that which is beyond or not physical.
